Report a Bug

If something is not working as expected, a few details help us fix it faster. When you email us, please include:

  • the tool you were using,
  • the letters or clue you entered,
  • what you expected to happen,
  • what happened instead,
  • your browser and device, if relevant.

Dictionary Corrections

Our tools use internal dictionaries that may not include every word, and occasionally a word may appear that you do not expect. If you find a missing word or an entry that looks wrong, let us know which tool and which word, and we will review it as we expand the word lists.

For advertising questions, partnership ideas, or other business inquiries, email us with a short description of what you have in mind and we will follow up.

Response Time

Word Puzzle Solver is a small, independent project, so replies may take some time. We read every message and appreciate your patience and your help making the tools better.

Privacy Policy

This page explains, in plain English, what information Word Puzzle Solver may collect and how cookies, advertising, and analytics may be used.

Effective date: June 18, 2026

Please note: This is a general website privacy policy written in plain language. It is provided for transparency and is not legal advice.

Word Puzzle Solver provides free, browser-based puzzle tools. You can use the site without creating an account. We aim to collect as little information as possible and to be clear about what is involved.

Information We Collect

You can use the site without signing up or providing personal details. We may collect basic, non-identifying usage information in the future through analytics — for example, which pages are popular or which tools are used most. We do not ask you to submit sensitive personal information.

Letters Entered Into Tools

When you use a tool such as the word unscrambler, the letters you enter are processed to produce results. This processing happens in your browser so the tool can show you matching words. We do not require you to provide your name, email, or other identifying details to use the tools.

Cookies and Advertising

We may display ads through Google AdSense or similar advertising partners. These partners may use cookies or similar technologies to serve and measure ads. You can disable or manage cookies through your browser settings at any time, though some features may behave differently if cookies are turned off.

Google AdSense

If Google AdSense is used to show ads, Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your visits to this and other websites. You can learn more about how Google uses information and manage your ad personalization settings through Google’s ad settings page at adssettings.google.com.

Analytics

We may use analytics tools in the future to understand how the site is used and to improve it. Analytics data is generally aggregated and used to measure traffic and performance rather than to identify individual visitors.

Email Communications

If you contact us by email, we may receive your email address and the contents of your message. We use this only to read and respond to your message. Our contact address is hello@thewordpuzzlesolver.com.

Third-Party Links

The site may link to third-party websites or services. Those third parties may collect data according to their own privacy policies. We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of websites we do not operate, so we encourage you to review their policies.

Children’s Privacy

The site is intended for general audiences and is not designed to knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can address it.

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to keep the site running safely. However, no website or internet transmission can be guaranteed to be completely secure, so we cannot promise absolute security of any information.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may change over time as the site grows or as our use of analytics and advertising evolves. When we make changes, we will update the effective date at the top of this page.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can reach us at hello@thewordpuzzlesolver.com.

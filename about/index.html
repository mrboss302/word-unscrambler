About the site

About Word Puzzle Solver

Word Puzzle Solver is a free collection of fast, browser-based tools for word games and letter puzzles — built to be simple, useful, and easy to use on any phone or laptop.

What We Build

Word Puzzle Solver is a growing set of free, browser-based tools for word games and letter puzzles. Everything runs right in your browser, so results appear instantly and there is nothing to install and no account to create.

The tools are made for people playing Scrabble-style games, Words With Friends-style games, Wordle-style games, crosswords, anagrams, spelling puzzles, and jumble puzzles. Whether you are stuck on a rack of tiles or just want to explore the words hidden in a set of letters, the goal is to help you find an answer quickly.

Why We Built It

Word games are more fun when you are not stuck. We wanted a set of tools that load fast, work well on a phone, and stay out of your way — no clutter, no sign-up walls, and no waiting.

The site is designed to be fast, simple, useful, and easy to use on mobile. Some tools rely on internal dictionaries, which means they may not include every possible word. We expand and refine these word lists over time to make results more complete.

Popular word game tools

Whether you play Scrabble, Words With Friends, Wordle, crosswords, or the daily Jumble, there is a tool here to get you unstuck. The word unscrambler and word solver are the go-to choices for turning a rack of letters into playable words, while the Wordle helper and 5 letter words finder are built for five-letter puzzles. Frequently asked questions

What is Word Puzzle Solver?

A free collection of fast, browser-based tools for word games and letter puzzles — a word unscrambler, word solver, Wordle helper, anagram generator, and a 5-letter words finder.

Which word game tools are available?

The Word Unscrambler, Word Solver, Wordle Helper, Anagram Generator, and 5 Letter Words finder are live. More tools, including a crossword solver, are planned.

Are the tools free?

Yes. Every tool is free, runs in your browser, and needs no account.

Do the tools work on mobile?

Yes. The site is mobile-first and works on phones, tablets, and desktops.

How to Use the Word Solver

  1. Enter your letters. Type the letters you have into “Available letters”.
  2. Add filters. Optionally require a letter, set a length, or filter by what the word starts with, ends with, or contains.
  3. Find words. Select Find Words to see matches grouped from longest to shortest.
  4. Scan and play. Each word shows its Scrabble score so you can pick the best play.

What Is a Word Solver?

A word solver turns a set of letters into the valid words you can build from them. It is broader than a basic unscrambler: alongside finding words from your letters, it lets you filter by prefix, suffix, contained letters, required letters, and length — so you can target exactly the kind of word you need.

If you only want to rearrange an exact set of tiles, the word unscrambler is a focused option. For five-letter puzzles, try the 5 letter words finder or the Wordle helper.

Find Words From Letters

Enter the letters you have and the solver checks each dictionary word to confirm you hold enough of every letter to spell it, respecting repeated tiles. If you leave the letters blank, you can still search the dictionary using only the starts-with, ends-with, and contains filters — handy for crossword clues where you know part of a word but not your full rack.

Word Solver for Scrabble and Words With Friends

Enter your seven tiles to see every legal word, ranked longest to shortest with its point value. Pair the length filter with the board: shorter words slot into tight spaces, while the longest results help you reach a bonus square. Scores use standard Scrabble values, so treat them as a strong guide rather than an exact Words With Friends total.

Word Solver Tips

Lead with length

Results are sorted longest-first because longer words usually score more and clear more tiles.

Use prefixes and suffixes

Common endings like -ing, -ed, and -er in the “ends with” field quickly surface playable forms.

Anchor with required letters

In a crossword, put a known letter in “must include” or “contains” to focus the list.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a word solver?

It is a tool that finds valid words from your letters, with filters to narrow results by length, required letters, and patterns.

How do I find words from letters?

Type your available letters and select Find Words. Add filters to focus the list.

Can I search by starting or ending letters?

Yes — use the starts with, ends with, and contains fields for prefix, suffix, and substring searches.

Can I use this for Scrabble?

Yes. Enter your rack to see playable words with Scrabble scores.

Are all results valid in every word game?

Not necessarily. Results come from a broad English word list and may include obscure words. They may not match every official game dictionary, and Word Puzzle Solver is not affiliated with any game publisher — confirm a word with your game’s official dictionary if needed.
